About Judge Ratios

The judge ratio (1:N) determines how many competitors each judge can accommodate:

  • Speech (1:5) - Each judge can handle 5 speech competitors
  • LD (1:2) - Each LD judge handles 2 debaters
  • PF (1:4) - Each PF judge handles 4 debaters (2 teams of 2)
  • Congress (1:8) - Each Congress judge handles 8 participants

These ratios are used when generating tournament rosters to determine how many competitors can participate based on available judges.
